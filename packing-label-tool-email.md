Subject: New tool for packing labels — quick walkthrough

Hey team,

We now have a tool for building packing lists and printing box labels. Here's how to use it:

1. Open [link] and bookmark it on the printer computer.
2. Click **+ Add Box** for each box in the shipment.
3. In the search field, type 3+ letters of the product name, SKU, or barcode. Results show up as you type — no Enter needed.
4. Click the product, enter the quantity, and click **Add to box**. Repeat for every product going in that box.
5. Add more boxes the same way until the whole shipment is built.
6. Click **Complete & Print Labels**. Each box prints two labels:
   - The first says "APPLY TO OUTSIDE OF BOX" — stick this on the outside.
   - The second has a bold **PLACE THIS LABEL INSIDE THE BOX** banner — drop this one inside before sealing it. That's our packing slip.

Made a mistake after printing? Go to the **History** tab, find the packing list by date, click **Reprint / Edit**, fix it, and print again.

Questions or something looks off, just let me know.
